How Live Data is Fetched

In our project Intellipark, we leverage the power of the YOLOv11 model to capture real-time data with high accuracy and speed. For demonstration purposes, we utilized live video streams of parking complexes sourced from YouTube. The YOLO model efficiently detects vehicles and identifies their availability within parking spaces, enabling seamless, real-time monitoring of occupancy.
